Officers

Bill Ottman, Founder & CEO: Bill is the driving force behind the vision, product development and creation of Minds. He is editor of multiple viral media organizations with millions of followers. He is also a writer, musician, artist and freedom of information activist.

John Ottman, Co-Founder & Chairman: John is Executive Chairman of Solix Technologies, Inc. and brings over 30 years of software industry experience to Minds. Previously, he was President and CEO of Application Security, Inc (Trustwave), President of Princeton Softech, Inc. (IBM), and Executive Vice President, Worldwide Markets at Corio, Inc. (IBM). He also has held key executive roles at Oracle, IBM and Wang Labs.

Mark Harding, Co-Founder & Chief Technology Officer: Mark is the lead engineer and also technical leader of the global Minds engineering community. Prior, Mark was Founder and CEO of Kramnorth. He is an orchestral musician, graduate of the Royal Conservatoire of Scotland and has been coding open-source systems for a decade.
